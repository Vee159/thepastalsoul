Resin Texture Techniques

Our artists use various methods to create captivating textures in resin:

  • Alcohol ink techniques for organic patterns
  • Embedded objects and mixed media
  • Silicone molds for dimensional texture
  • Hand-carved resin surfaces
  • Thermal shock cracking effects

Meet the Artist

Kinjal Parajapati is a contemporary resin artist specializing in innovative texture techniques that transform epoxy resin into captivating visual experiences.

Her work blends traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques, often incorporating natural pigments, metallic foils, and layered textures.

"My resin art journey began as an expression of my soul's connection with colors and textures. Each piece tells a story through its depth and dimension, creating unique visual poetry in resin."
